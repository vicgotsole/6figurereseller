Hi, I’m Victor — founder of 6 Figure Reseller.

Over the last 12 years, I’ve built a reselling business that has generated more than $5,000,000 in sales on eBay alone. What started as a side hustle quickly became a full-time career, and along the way I learned exactly what it takes to create a consistent, scalable, and profitable reselling business.

Like many resellers, I started small—selling items I had around the house and flipping finds from thrift stores. Over time, I refined my systems for sourcing, listing, pricing, and shipping until I was running a streamlined operation that produced predictable results. Those lessons weren’t learned overnight; they came through trial, error, and the discipline to treat reselling like a real business instead of a hobby.

Now, my mission is to help other resellers shorten that learning curve. I know firsthand how overwhelming it can feel when you’re stuck guessing what to list, how to price, or how to handle fees and inventory. That’s why I created this coaching program—to share my proven playbook, provide accountability, and give resellers the confidence to build something that lasts.

Whether you’re just starting out or looking to scale beyond part-time income, I’m here to help you hit your goals. Reselling changed my life, and I believe it can change yours too—with the right guidance, systems, and support. Together, we’ll take the guesswork out of growing your eBay business and put you on a clear path to becoming a 6 Figure Reseller.
