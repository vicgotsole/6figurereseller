The Starting Point

Marcus was a high-performing sales rep in Atlanta working long hours and traveling frequently. He wanted a change of lifestyle—more control over his time and a business he could scale without burning out. He’d dabbled in flipping but lacked a consistent sourcing pipeline, listing system, and pricing strategy.

The Plan

Marcus enrolled in our Elite coaching plan and added $1,000 in monthly wholesale inventory to stabilize his pipeline. Together we built a 12-week roadmap focused on inventory quality, listing velocity, and operations so he could transition from side-hustle to business.

Core Moves We Implemented

  • Inventory mix & comps: Focused on 3 fast-turn categories with ≥3× resale based on historic comps.
  • Listing velocity system: Daily target with batching, templates, and scheduled photo blocks to 2× output.
  • Pricing & offers: Framework for launches, markdown cadence, and automated offers to watchers.
  • Ops & margin: Shipping presets, return policy that protects margin, and weekly KPI review.
  • Wholesale subscription: $1,000/mo curated lots to reduce sourcing time and keep shelves full.

Execution Timeline

  1. Weeks 1–4: Store audit, category focus, and listing templates. Hit first consistent 7-day streaks.
  2. Weeks 5–8: Pricing/offer cadence + photo batching. Sales velocity climbs; cash flow steadies.
  3. Weeks 9–12: Storage layout, SKU system, and shipping presets. Time per order drops 35%.
  4. Months 6–9: Adds VA for listing prep; expands wholesale allotment; negotiates better unit costs.
  5. Month 14: Signs lease on a 3,000 sq ft warehouse and exits his sales job.

Results

  • Full-time in 14 months with a dedicated warehouse and weekly intake schedule.
  • Consistent pipeline: Wholesale + targeted local sourcing to smooth out sales dips.
  • Profit trajectory: On pace to exceed $200,000 profit by end of Year 2.
  • Owner time: Moved from nights/weekends to a structured 40-hr week with clear KPIs.
