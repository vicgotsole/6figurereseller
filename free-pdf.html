50+ household items

A room-by-room checklist of things you likely already own that can be turned into quick cash. Each item in the guide includes plain-English examples so you can scan the house once and come back with a stack to list the same day.

  • Kitchen: vintage Pyrex, cast-iron pans, brand-name knives, sealed small-appliance parts, water filters.
  • Closet: name-brand denim, techwear, boots, belts, hats, team gear, vintage tees.
  • Garage: power tools, battery chargers, Carhartt/Workwear, camping gear, bike parts, coolers.
  • Gadgets: old iPods/MP3 players, calculators, cameras, gaming controllers, remotes.
